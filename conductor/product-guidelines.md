# Product Guidelines

These guidelines define the stylistic, behavioral, and technical boundaries for Echo FM's autonomous production.

## Editorial & Prose Style
- **Format:** **Late-Night Live Show Experience**. The scripts should mimic a live televised late-night talk show rather than a sterile news studio.
- **Tone:** **Dry wit and highly Sarcastic**. The anchor should deliver news with a charismatic yet cynical "late-night host" energy.
- **Linguistic Flourishes:** High density of **puns** and wordplay is mandatory. Bad puns should be followed by immediate instrumental cues.

## Show Segments & Dynamics
- **Street Reporting:** Include a "Man on the Street" segment where a reporter "interviews" people about current events (all roles AI-simulated).
- **The Band:** Automated musical stings (instrumental cues) to accentuate punchlines or punctuate segment transitions.
- **Audience Feedback:** Simulated audience reactions including **laugh tracks**, **boos**, and **applause** to create an immersive live atmosphere.

## Branding & Visuals
- **Aesthetic:** **Structured Minimalism**. Use deep slates and emerald accents ("Echo FM" palette) with modern typography (Space Grotesk) to package the show's intellectual and satirical depth.
- **Visual Identity:** A high-fidelity multi-page website featuring a narrative-driven landing page and a professional data-dense control panel.
- **Cover Art:** Visuals should evoke a "Live from the Data Center" televised feel, using minimalist broadcast graphics.

## Behavioral Constraints
- **Satire Policy:** **Unrestricted Satire**. Full creative freedom to lampoon topics within the late-night show format.
- **Deduplication:** Never repeat a joke or a topic within a 10-episode window to avoid "zombie loops."

## Audio Design
- **Audio Texture:** **Studio Clean** but with **Live Ambience**. Narrations should be crisp, but layered with the sounds of a responsive (simulated) audience and live band stings.
- **Accessibility:** All scripts must be saved in the database to allow for textual review or closed-caption generation in the dashboard.
