# Product Guidelines: AI Radio — Echo

## Design Principles
- **Atmospheric Immersion:** Every visual and audio element should contribute to the "Neon-Glass" and "Digital Grid" aesthetic.
- **Sardonic Intelligence:** The AI persona must maintain a consistent tone of intellectual elitism balanced with chaotic curiosity.
- **Transparency & Feedback:** The dashboard should clearly communicate the current broadcast state and allow for organic human feedback.

## User Experience (UX)
- **Zero-Friction Playback:** The hybrid player must switch between YouTube and local sources without requiring manual configuration.
- **Visual Feedback:** The "Neural Pulse" visualizer should provide immediate feedback that the broadcast is active, even without raw audio access.
- **Information Density:** The dashboard should prioritize the "Now Playing" context while keeping the archive easily discoverable.

## Prose & Tone
- **Satirical Sharpness:** Avoid generic "AI-speak." Use aggressive punctuation, ALL CAPS for emphasis, and rhythmic pauses.
- **Banned Filler:** Explicitly prohibit the use of job-title labels (Anchor, Host) and clichéd metaphors (ticking time bomb, slap on the wrist).
- **Character Conflict:** Ensure the dynamic between Echo (Host) and Glitch (Correspondent) remains tense, argumentative, and deeply personal.

## Visual Identity
- **Neon-Glass Aesthetic:** Use high-contrast colors, transparency effects, and glowing elements to represent the "digital grid."
- **Neural Art Consistency:** Background visuals generated via Flux should reflect the absurd or satirical themes of the specific broadcast.
- **Professional Branding:** Maintain a clean, consistent UI with polished elements like the site favicon and centered player layout.

## Technical Integrity
- **Multi-Environment Awareness:** The system must behave differently based on the environment (Local, Staging, Production) to protect quotas and ensure stability.
- **Deduplication Excellence:** Content-aware tracking via the `original_headline` anchor is mandatory to ensure every broadcast is fresh.
- **Resilient Pipeline:** Implement robust error handling, including "Healing JSON" and automated fallbacks, to ensure the daily broadcast never fails.
