# Product Definition: AI Radio — Echo

## Vision
To provide deep-dive satirical news commentary through a fully autonomous audio-visual pipeline, treating human affairs with the "playful cynicism of a scientist watching ants fight over a discarded potato chip."

## Target Audience
- Digital citizens seeking witty and sarcastic news perspectives.
- Fans of "Late Night" satire (Jon Stewart/Colbert style).
- Developers interested in autonomous AI pipelines.

## Core Value Proposition
A seamless, automated broadcast system that transforms dry news into high-quality, emotionally resonant satirical content, complete with unique visuals and an engaging dashboard.

## Key Features
- **Intelligent Ingestion:** Automated scraping from RSS feeds and HackerNews with threshold-based deduplication.
- **Satirical Brain:** AI-generated scripts using Llama 3.3 and Gemini 3.5 Flash, focusing on mono-topic deep dives.
- **Emotional Performance:** High-fidelity TTS synthesis using Groq Orpheus with advanced prosody and character dynamics (Echo & Glitch).
- **Neural Art:** Unique HD background visuals generated for every broadcast via Flux (Pollinations.ai).
- **Hybrid Hub:** A professional web dashboard supporting both YouTube production embeds and local offline file playback.
- **Multi-Environment Firewall:** Isolated logic and database persistence for Production, Staging, and Local development.

## Success Metrics
- **Broadcast Consistency:** Successful daily autonomous generation and distribution.
- **Content Quality:** Meeting the 600-second minimum duration and maintaining character persona integrity.
- **System Stability:** Passing all health checks and verification suites across environments.
